Join us for two days of talks, codelabs, and breakout sessions from the Polymer team, Googlers, and major companies using Polymer and Web Components.

Schedule

We are planning a full schedule of technical talks for both days, plus an onsite afterparty Tuesday night. Onsite attendees will also have unprecedented access to Google engineering teams during the event.

When and Where

Getting There

Parking

There will be limited car parking available onsite. We encourage visitors to take public transportation to the venue.

Mainline Rail

The nearest main train S-station is Dybbølsbro and is an 8 minute walk / 4 minute bike ride to the venue.

Travelling by bus, train, or the metro? Plan your journey ahead through the Rejseplanen website/app.

Airports

Copenhagen airport is a short 15 minute drive away from the venue.

Taxis

There are no taxi ranks located at the venue, but these can be booked via their website, app, or by calling them on +45 35 35 35 35.

Events & Amenities.

After Hours

Attendees are invited to an after party at the end of the first day, on 22 August from 18:00-22:00.
